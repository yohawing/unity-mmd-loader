# Humanoid Playback sample assets

This is a ready-to-play quality sample. Open `Assets/HumanoidPlayback.unity` and
use the `Humanoid Timeline` PlayableDirector. Its single Humanoid track plays the
39.733-second animation embedded in `Assets/TaisouMocap.fbx`.

## `Assets/TaisouMocap.fbx`

- Original filename: `taisou_mocap.fbx`
- Provided by: the unity-mmd-loader project maintainer
- Added: 2026-07-12
- SHA-256: `972E72E6AF8B0C7D32B4762EB7180395BCF6BAC03ADCF5BE27EA95AE8B655753`
- Permission: the provider explicitly confirmed that this already-captured
  motion is free material and may be redistributed with this package sample
- Use: imported as a Unity Humanoid animation and played through
  `MmdHumanoidAnimationTrack`

This practical FBX motion is the sample's motion-quality example. The short
`HumanoidSampleMotion.vmd` used by contract fixtures is not bundled here and is
only for conversion-contract checks; it is not evidence of natural motion
quality.
